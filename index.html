Spicy Pasta
Primavera

This delightful pasta dish combines rich roasted tomatoes with al dente spaghetti, creating a perfect symphony of taste and texture.

Method
Stovetop
Time
45 Minutes
Portion
2 Servings
Calories
320 kcal
Steps

Spicy Pasta Primavera

1

Prepare Ingredients

Gather all ingredients: 400g spaghetti, 3 cups cherry tomatoes, 2 cloves garlic, fresh basil, olive oil, salt, and pepper.

2

Cook the Pasta

Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Add spaghetti and cook according to package directions until al dente.

3

Prepare the Sauce

While pasta cooks, halve the cherry tomatoes. Sauté garlic in olive oil until fragrant, then add tomatoes.

4

Combine and Serve

Drain pasta and toss with the tomato sauce. Season with salt, pepper, and fresh basil. Serve immediately.
